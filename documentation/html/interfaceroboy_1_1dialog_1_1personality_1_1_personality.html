Detailed Description

Personality interface. A personality is designed to define how roboy reacts in every given situation. Roboy can always only represent one personality at a time. Different personalities are meant to be used in different situations, like a more formal or loose one depending on the occasion where he is at. In the future, also different languages could be realized by the use of different personalities.
